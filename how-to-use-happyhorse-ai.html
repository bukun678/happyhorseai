How to Use HappyHorse AI Video Generator

Learn how to use HappyHorse AI Video Generator step by step, from writing better prompts to choosing style, ratio, and duration.

How to use HappyHorse AI step by step

  1. Open the HappyHorse AI Generator.
  2. Enter a clear video prompt with subject, motion, camera angle, and mood.
  3. Choose a style such as Cinematic, Commercial, Anime, Product Ad, or Social Clip.
  4. Select the aspect ratio that matches your use case, such as 16:9 or 9:16.
  5. Set a duration and click Generate Video.
  6. Review the result and iterate with tighter prompts for better output.

Best prompt formula

A simple prompt formula makes the generator easier to use:

Subject + environment + motion + camera + lighting + style

Example: glowing horse running through a futuristic city, dramatic tracking shot, neon reflections, cinematic lighting, high detail

Tips for better results

  • Keep the subject consistent in every prompt.
  • Use camera words like close-up, wide shot, or tracking shot.
  • Specify motion, not just appearance.
  • For short-form content, switch to 9:16.
